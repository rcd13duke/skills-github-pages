My teaching prioritizes fostering independent, yet collaborative, critical thinking. This is achieved through a cooperative classroom environment, the inclusion of diverse voices, and interactive methods that challenge students to consider multiple perspectives. In doing so, my aim is to increase the intellectual horizons of students and so strengthen their capacity to make meaningful contributions to the communities in which they operate.
